What We Do

Research

We provide clients with credible research about their target market and identify both direct and indirect competitors within their business environment.

Strategy Advice

We help businesses identify clear, specific strategic change points that need to be implemented in order to turn their situation around.

Start-up Services

We provide start-up entrepreneurs with core services which they need in order to assess the feasibility of, and need for their business offering, gain a deep understanding of their business environment.

Financial Management

These services assist existing business owners to plan for their financial commitments, manage their cash flows.

Who we are

Naa Adjorkor Adjei (Jay)

Jay is an optimist who is passionate about the wave of entrepreneurship sweeping across Africa. Her vision is to assist business owners to reach their full potential by offering them comprehensive business solutions, including financial models, business plans, market research and business turnaround strategies.

The value she brings to your business is her ability to quickly identify problems and opportunities through the lens of her unique background and experiences. She applies a collaborative approach to working with clients in order understand their distinctive business offering within the context of their competitive environment. Furthermore she develops workable solutions and strategies that meet their specific needs, based on sound, globally proven business principles.

The first five years of her career were spent with global manufacturing giant, Toyota. Here, she was responsible for a budget of over half a billion rands for various manufacturing areas and was involved in strategy development and implementation within the business, where she reported directly to the Vice President of Finance. She has since progressed towards an exciting career in the global financial markets working at information leader Bloomberg, with a key focus on managing and reporting on the market expectations for the performance of listed equities in the EMEA region, applying her vast knowledge of macroeconomic factors and industry performance drivers.

A student of the dynamic field on entrepreneurship in her own right, Jayâ€™s first stint as an entrepreneur was managing a public phone business and selling cosmetic products while in university, thereafter going on to operate a successful import and distribution business.

Jay holds a Bachelor of Commerce Honours from the University of Cape Town, specialising in the areas of Financial Analysis and Portfolio Management. In addition, she holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Accounting, Managerial Accounting and Finance as well as a Post Graduate Diploma in Financial Planning.

In her spare time, Jay enjoys reading, socializing and exploring the beautiful city of Cape Town.

Mapaseka Dipale

Mapaseka Dipale is social entrepreneur who is passionate about education, technology, start-ups, open-data and helping government leverage innovation. She thrives in helping people reach new heights. She is an innovative thinker and business analyst with knowledge of financial markets, equity crowdfunding and data analysis.

Mapaseka started her career at African Ideas as an analyst, were she was assigned to the Western Cape Broadband Strategy and Readiness Advisory project. The complexity of this project cut across multiple departments at Provincial Government, Local Government and National Government level, which created the need to contract consultants and a wide variety of external stakeholders in order to achieve successful implementation. She was specifically responsible for looking at internet penetration at schools in the Western Cape, population density and income levels per ward in order to develop a heat map for the Western Cape government to inform key intervention areas.

She then joined a donation crowdfunding company were her main responsibility was to analyse donors trends and communicate with campaign owners providing strategic direction for marketing their campaigns. She then consulted for an equity crowdfunding platform, were her focus was advising on payment gateways solutions, improving online presence and was the main liaison between the investors and entrepreneurs seeking equity funding.

Mapaseka has also worked for a leading financial and market news company as an analyst, which has allowed her harness her research skills and understanding of macroeconomic impact on listed companies.

The value she brings to your business is her ability to identify gaps, perform situation analysis and leverage technology to provide solutions that are tailored for the company she is working with. Mapaseka holds a Bachelor of Commerce (Rationum) in Computer Science and Information Systems from Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University (NMMU).

Mapaseka is a highly motivated person who is driven by positive results, thus her involvement in projects that help foster a community of entrepreneurship. In this capacity, she has been involved with various community impact projects and organisations were her involvement has ranged from organising a tech scholarsâ€™ conference for 150 high school learners in Cape Town, continued mentorship of scholars in her personal capacity and volunteering for initiatives to improve education. She is an organising member of Net Prophet Spark Up, an accelerator for tech start-ups based in the Western Cape. She is also involved in PE Think Tank which was designed to build a vibrant, sustainable entrepreneurial ecosystem and culture in Port Elizabeth. She has also organised, hosted and participated Hackathons, with two wins to her name.
