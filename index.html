About CLR Women

Crowdfunding

The CLR Women platform allows women to invest in female-owned businesses in exchange for equity.

Crowdsourcing

The platform allows entrepreneurs to obtain skills needed by their business.

Donations

Women are encouraged to donate to various organizations we work with aimed at empowering women.

Business

All businesses that are listed on CLR Women are 100% female owned and are in operation.

Investors

Only women are allowed to invest in the businesses listed on CLR Women.
